WHAT IS JUSTIFIABLE?

and does it correlate with education level?

Project 2 by Bereg. Data from WVSbetween 1995-2014.


First analytic trail

I started by looking at the WVS website and decided to investigate what the world thought was justifiable. I also wanted to see if that was correlated with the highest educational level in that country. My hypothesis was that countries with higher education level would think that e.g. homosexuality and abortion are justifiable. I selected the latest wave (2010-2014) and randomly choose the countries as shown below.
